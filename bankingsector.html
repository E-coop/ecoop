BANKING SECTOR IMPACTS

Increases the effectiveness of the internal organization

One of the first banks to push this forth was, Bank of America.They use high quality video and sound for meetings between staff.It allows banks to have meeting regardless of the location of their staff.Thus saving time. Telepresence does not only allow for videoconferencing, but it also allows for interactions. With this technology, the bank made it possible for customers to interact with mortgage specialists, financial advisors and small business bankers from the comfort of their homes or offices. This technological advancement was put in place because the bank recognised the changing shape of the consumer banking landscape

Gain expert opinion or diagnosis globally

The system gives patients access to specialists across the world.Limited physician and clinician resources can now be scaled across multiple locations to provide consistent, optimized levels of quality care. Specialized diagnoses and face-to-face consultations can be delivered cost-effectively and more frequently. Hospitals and specialty centers that are linked using Cisco TelePresence can take advantage of scarce resources, such as ICU physician monitoring, virtually.

Improve Customer Service
Telepresence allows the bank to extend their services beyond the walls of their institution for their potential and current customers. Thiustomer choice and brought advice directly to customers.

Increases Customer Engagement

Telepresence is both advantageous to the bank and the customer, in relation to banks, it can reduce branch foot traffic, and increase the use of digital forums. It increases the efficiency and effectiveness of the respective branch.For the customers,they do not have to wait in frustrating long lines to get advice on banking.
